I. The Asset Philosophy

Every POXY is an immutable cryptographic entity. From the precise nanosecond of its generation, each asset carries a permanent, unalterable chain of custody — a digital fingerprint that follows the asset through every transaction, trade, and transfer in perpetuity. No two POXY share identical provenance. Each serial code is a unique gateway into the historical record of an asset's complete life cycle.

Ownership is not a permission granted by a platform. It is a verifiable mathematical fact recorded on an immutable ledger. Your POXY is not rented, licensed, or conditionally granted. It belongs to you in the most absolute digital sense possible.

The rarity tiers — Common through Mythic — are not arbitrary labels. They represent genuine scarcity coefficients baked permanently into the generation algorithm. A Mythic is mathematically certified to have a 1-in-10,000 emergence probability, making each one a statistically extraordinary event by computational definition.
