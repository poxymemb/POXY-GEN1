help FAQ

What is Air-Gap on OTC?

After a new login, peer transfers freeze for 24 hours to protect your vault from session hijack.

Can I hide my balance when someone opens my passport link?

Yes — enable Hide PC balance on public passport. Rank and showcase remain visible.

What does shield level change?

Higher levels add OTC confirmations and stricter sharing. Maximum requires a saved recovery key before large OTC moves.

Is guest passport view available?

Planned for verified club members only. Until then, viewers must sign in to open passport links.

How do I feature a POXY on my passport?

Open Collection, pin a POXY as featured, then check Passport or Club Profile.
